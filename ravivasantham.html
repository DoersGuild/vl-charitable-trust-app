RAVI VASANTHAM- The Sevagram

"WE MAKE A LIVING BY WHAT WE GET, BUT WE MAKE A LIFE BY WHAT WE GIVE"

"Our unique project"

The concept of “RAVI VASANTHAM - The Sevagram” is to combine the services and activities of the trust to the women, children, disabled and old age people. To provide services in a holistic manner under one roof through different units,

Athmeya – for Orphans,

Shakthi – for Women empowerment,

Alambana – for Old age people,

Samrakshna – A rehabilitation center for M.R Children,

Sanjeevani – A Free Multi Specialty hospital to provide care and cure for the poor and needy,

Saparya – A Palliative end care for old age people.

Together we make a complete home.

“Home for the homeless and family for the family less with love and compassion”
