OUR STRENGTH

Having completed a little over two decades of service through a wide range of humanitarian activities Vasantha Lakshmi Charitable Trust & Research Center has gone through the mill initially, surpassed in performance many sister organizations with similar aims & objectives earned its considered actions with great success.

Nonetheless, it is not out of place to mention not only to gain high visibility for its socially useful work and activities but also for introspective stock taking and incisive self-evaluation.

Though much is achieved more remains on the horizon. Message and opinions of eminent persons and parents and public alike show that Vasantha Lakshmi Charitable Trust & Research Center not only come out of age but has bloomed into a vibrant organization.

In this connection, a word about Dr. T. Ravi Kumar, M.D., D.C.H (Pead.) Founder Trustee and Smt. T. Vasantha Lakshmi, Chairperson of the Trust is imminent..

Their quite efficiency, wide vision and total commitment are solely responsible for accelerated raise of the institution in a short span of time..

All these achievements could not have been possible had there being no unstinted support extended by the Honorable Members of the Governing Body..
