SANJEEVENI: A 100 BEDED FREE MULTI SUPER SPECIALITY HOSPITAL

MOTTO : Provide hygiene comfort and safe medical care for their well being healing with a human touch, love and compassion and strive to always uphold it.











VISION:

Provide High quality comprehensive care to all, particularly of the needs of the vulnerable populations through our integrated delivery system in an ethically responsible manner with touch of humanity love and compassion.

MISSION:

To serve the health care needs to the medically unreached populations with quality medical, paramedical, nursing and allied healthcare education, excellent and compassionate services with state of the art facilities and to carry out health research for the betterment of the human kind in tune with the holistic values of the sevagram

"SANJEEVANI” gives utmost priority to the comfort and early recovery of the patients. We are focusing on quick registration and discharge, least waiting time in OP and pharmacy, maximum nursing attention and least botheration for the patients and bystanders to avail any service. Cleanliness is also looked into very seriously, both for aesthetic appearance as well as medical requirement."Hospital built with Love as essence, heart full of compassion and being humanity as its walls ", is a 100 bedded multi-specialty hospital, which is being guided and operated by senior doctors themselves. We help the patient in every possible way, to the extent that all needy and poor patients get free treatment even if it is in the form of operation to cure him from disease. Our charges of Love and Compassion with dignity and humanity are the view of the quality of service, and comfort we provide to the patients. All our panelist doctors are having working experience of more than 15 years. We do have all necessary State-of-the-Art equipments for good treatment.

SANJEEVENI has established itself as the best hospitals in and around Nellore District with a mission to provide world class integrated healthcare facilities to all sections of the society with a humanitarian touch, whilst maintaining high standard of ethical practice and professional competency with emphasis on training and education leading to research. "The Institute will impart free Medicare to the poor, needy and deserving people of the weaker sections and underprivileged communities bearing the only cause for the service of mankind.

SHELTER:

"SANJEEVANI” state-of-the-art Multispeciality Hospital with 100 beds more emphasis is given on Patient – Doctor relationship & one to one interaction. Doctors, Support staff of Sanjeevani aims at making Sanjeevani a multi facility hospital which would be the best of its kinds in Nellore District coming days."SANJEEVANI” hospital aspires to be a highly effective professional organization devoted to ensuring and striving to improve the health status of all the deprived, deserted, old aged, underprivileged etc of our community."SANJEEVANI” hospital is a premier healthcare that strives to anticipate and respond to lifelong needs and beyond by providing excellent clinical care.
