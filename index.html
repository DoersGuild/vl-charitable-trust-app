Welcome to Vasantha Lakshmi Charitable Trust

The roots of the true achievement lie in the will to become the best that you can become. In a bid to provide solace and shelter to the fractured hopes of disadvantaged groups in the society and to foster its role to keep a pace with nerve racking speed the world is changing with, the Vasantha Lakshmi Charitable Trust & Research Center, Nellore established in the year 1993, under Indian Trust Act 1882 Registered in the year 1998, Regd No.82/98 by renowned pediatrician of Nellore District Dr. T. Ravi Kumar., M.D., D.C.H (Pead.).

Recognizing the ideology of social service as espoused and practiced by Smt. T. Vasantha Lakshmi, M.Com.., Spl. B.Ed (M.R)., L.L.B on whose name the Trust was founded has resolved to launch a wide range of social welfare and development activities imbibed with two distinct objectives vis-a-vis Social Service and Human Development to carry out necessary architectural correction to improve the quality of life of the most disadvantaged groups of the society. The Mission adopts a synergistic approach "to reach the unreached" areas of Nellore District of Andhra Pradesh, South India. Having been in this noble work for the last two Decades Vasantha Lakshmi Charitable Trust & Research Center could venture to expand its services to the Kadapa and Ongole districts of Andhra Pradesh.
