VISION & MISSION


MOTTO: “TO REACH THE UNREACHED AND TO SERVE THE UNSERVED”

MISSION: The Mission of VLCT & RC is to enable an interfaith and community response to the deprived, destitute women, orphans homeless by providing shelter, food, comprehensive assistance and encouragement towards self-reliance in an atmosphere of warmth and hospitality and committed to serve the community to provide the homeless a fresh start to rebuild their lives beyond shelters created with Humanity, Love and Compassion .

HOME is not build with 4 walls instead filled with HUMANITY, LOVE, COMPASSION and RELATIONSHIPS

  • Be treated fairly, equally to lead their own lives with pride and respect
  • To provide safe living, appropriate, well-maintained surroundings
  • Have their privilege and dignity maintained
  • Acceptance and understanding without any discrepancies and queries.

VISION:  There is much suffering in the world both physical and mental. The physical and mental suffering are suffering from hunger, from homelessness, from mental disability, from physically challenged, from all kinds of diseases etc. But the greatest suffering is being, neglected, destitute, lonely, feeling unloved, having no one and no shelter. So we want to create a world of love and care without any discrimination.

“Humanity, love and compassion in actions transforming lives in reactions”

Bestow pride is the essence of “RAVIVASANTHAM” the sevagram in a holistic manner entirely designed and equipped environment through various services providing and environment of “ECO-FRIENDLY AND DISABLE FRIENDLY”.
