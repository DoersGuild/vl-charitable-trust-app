SAPARYA: A HOME OF PALLAVTIVE END CARE FOR OLD AGE PEOPLE

MOTTO : Improve the quality of life of patients and committed to deliver and demonstrate excellence in everything we do through comfort, Love and Dignity.
CARE BEYOUND CURE











VISION:

Our vision is that the SAPARYA for Palliative Care will be a regional and national leader in palliative care for the benefit of all patients, families, caregivers, and policy makers. SAPARYA offers leadership in the pursuit of excellence in care for persons approaching death so that the burdens of suffering, loneliness and grief are lessened.

MISSION:

The mission of SAPARYA, home for Palliative Care is to relieve suffering and improve quality of life for patients with advanced disease and their families through humanity, Love, compassionate and high quality care and help them discover life’s most important moments. We are not just a provider of services, but together we capture moments that really matter which is why we call this as ‘patient-focused care’.

To advance the field of palliative care through innovation, education and research.

The burgeoning influences of the society have made people busy in their day to day scheduled work giving less importance for their age old parents and the geriatrics that need special care and touch of love. VLCT & RC has initiated a unique project called ”RAVIVASANTHAM - the sevagram which caters all the needs of this dying old aged people through SAPARYA with care values..

  • Commitment:Committed first and foremost to the delivery of Compassionate Care for all of our constituencies (patients, families, community, and each other).
  • Compassion:Compassionate Care is at the very heart of what we do and why we exist. Which values human dignity?
  • Competence :Competent Care is the first essential step to providing Compassionate Care. It is leading edge and cost competitive with any other credible provider.
  • Community :Community support is our ultimate edge in providing Compassionate Care. It enables us to constructively enlist the collaborative support of volunteers and other community entities, creating the capacity to meet community needs.
  • Creativity:Creativity is tapping the brilliance within us and our community to generate ideas that will continually improve Compassionate Care in the face of all that might challenge it.

Palliative care is an approach that improves the quality of life of patients and their families facing the problem associated with life-threatening illness, through the prevention and relief of suffering by means of early identification and impeccable assessment and treatment of pain and other problems, physical, psychosocial and spiritual. Palliative care:

  • provides relief from pain and other distressing symptoms;
  • Affirms life and regards dying as a normal process;
  • Intends neither to hasten or postpone death;
  • Integrates the psychological and spiritual aspects of patient care;
  • Offers a support system to help patients live as actively as possible until death;
  • Offers a support system to help the family cope during the patients illness and in their own bereavement;
  • Uses a team approach to address the needs of patients and their families, including bereavement counselling, if indicated;
  • Will enhance quality of life, and may also positively influence the course of illness;
  • Is applicable early in the course of illness, in conjunction with other therapies that are intended
  • Is applicable early in the course of illness, in conjunction with other therapies that are intended
    to prolong life, such as chemotherapy or radiation therapy, and includes those investigations needed to better understand and manage distressing clinical complications.
